The Four Phase Program...

        Presently, the Club's main aim is to create a general awareness about astronomy among students and to transform one from a beginner to an amateur astronomer, sometimes even a professional astronomer. All the activities of the club are done to achieve these goals. But, if a person wants to work seriously in the practical field then he/she needs some basic infrastructure. Therefore, our club has planned to setup a permanent observatory. This involves the following projects:-

1. Building an 8" reflector telescope
2. Attaching a motor drive to the telescope
3. Building an observatory for housing the above telescope
4. CCD attachment and Automatic control
