Welcome to the Astronomy Club

The ASTRONOMY CLUB is part of the Science and Technology Council of IIT Kanpur. Here, we attempt to bring about an interest and awareness in the field of Astronomy and Space Research.

We are involved in various activities. Some of these are: -

  • Observation Sessions are organized by the club to introduce new members to the basic of sky gazing. Beginners are taught to handle telescope, read star charts, and the more advanced members go for deep sky objects- Galaxies, Star clusters, Nebulae etc. Even naked eye astronomy can provide considerable enjoyment and using a telescope better details of planets and star clusters can be seen.

  • Slide show and Lectures- Club also organizes slide shows in the semester to give a feel of the subject. Lectures on the various motions in the sky and general astronomy are held, too. There are lectures held on mythology related to constellations, new developments in Astronomy etc.

  • Astrophotography- We also take photograph of constellations, planets, moon and other deep sky objects. We have often observed solar and lunar eclipses and taken photos of them.
