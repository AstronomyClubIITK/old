Links

Some of the best in the field of Astronomy...


NASA
     Requires no introduction. Provides information about anything you can think on in Astronomy. See this website for updates related to various manned missions to International Space Station etc, as well as progress of unmanned probes like Mars Rovers etc.

Space.com
     Space.com is a space and astronomy news website. Its stories are often syndicated to other media outlets. A very informative and up to date site providing latest information about space and all you need to know about it. Also had a large discussion board. Has a large set of articles which discuss advances made in spaceflight and astronomy.

Sky and Telescope
     Apart from giving periodicals on news and events it provides invaluable tips on imaging and also displays latest astronomical kits. The definitive guide for an amateur astronomer's introduction to the night sky. The articles are intended for the informed lay reader and include detailed discussions of current discoveries, frequently by participating scientists. Sky & Telescope is illustrated in full color, with both amateur and professional photography of celestial sights, as well as tables and charts of upcoming celestial events.

Students for the exploration and development of space (SEDS)
     SEDS is a student based organization which is dedicated to promoting the exploration and development of space. SEDS pursues this mission by educating people about the benefits of space, by supporting a network of interested students, by providing an opportunity for members to develop their leadership skills, and inspiring people through our involvement in space-related projects Very rich site providing information solar system, constellations and also deep space bodies like Messier objects and NGC catalog.

Hubble Space Telescope
     This site reveals information about Hubble Space Telescope(HST) and Next Generation Space Telescope(NGST). It has got a wealthy collection of deep space images taken by HST.

Astronomy Magazine
     A popular site publishing weekly news about recent events in astronomy.

Star Mythology
     Many amateur specially beginners find the mythology behind the constellations much more amusing than anything else in practical astronomy. This site solves their purpose to a very great extent.

European Space Agency
     Nice collection of articles and news. The glossary might also prove very useful to you.
