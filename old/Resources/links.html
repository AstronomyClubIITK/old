Useful Links

Some of the best in the field of Astronomy...


NASA
     Requires no introduction. Provides information about anything you can think on in Astronomy.

Sky and Telescope
     Apart from giving periodicals on news and events it provides invaluable tips on imaging and also displays latest astronomical kits.

Students for the exploration and development of space
     Very rich site providing information solar system, constellations and also deep space bodies like Messier objects and NGC catalog.

American Association of Amateur Astronomers
     This is very rich site providing information about facts and data pages. It has also got very rich CCD image gallery.

Hubble Space Telescope
     This site reveals information about Hubble Space Telescope(HST) and Next Generation Space Telescope(NGST). It has got a wealthy collection of deep space images taken by HST.

Messier Objects
     This is an excellent site having good pictures on Messier Objects specially the open clusters.

Astronomy Magazine
     A popular site publishing weekly news about recent events in astronomy.

Star Mythology
     Many amateur specially beginners find the mythology behind the constellations much more amusing than anything else in practical astronomy. This site solves their purpose to a very great extent.

European Space Agency
     Nice collection of articles and news. The glossary might also prove very useful to you.
