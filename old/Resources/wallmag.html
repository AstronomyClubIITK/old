The Astronomy Club has been publishing its Wall magazine - "Cygnus" in the past few years.
Students and faculty contribute articles for it which may include serious Astronomical stuff to Astronomy Jokes and almost anything.
Moreover we maintain an archive of articles which, the Faculty and student members write on various topics related to the field.
This year we are introducing a special section in the articles for the beginners. These articles focus the articles for the beginners. These articles focus on details of things related to the Observational Astronomy and also on the mythology, which makes the things a lot more interesting and enjoyable for beginners.
